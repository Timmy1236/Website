# Hello?

I am not going to lie to you and I never would. But, honestly, I am not a fan of writing these kinds of "things" that people call "blog". And I am serious! Totally from my heart! <p style="color:red; font-size:20px">**I AM CAPABLE OF RIPPING IT OUT OF MY CHEST SO YOUR OWN EYES CAN SEE IT!**</p>

<br>

... For other normal people, writing a simple text just expressing themselves is an everyday task that they can perfectly do at any moment whenever they feel like it, but, for me... It feels impossible.

* **I have never** been good at talking with other people verbally.

* **I have never** been good at expressing my thoughts or feelings.

<br>

In the first place I am not capable of really knowing what I am thinking *What?*. I am capable of imagining a lot of fascinating things that I would like to do and say, but at the moment of "grabbing it" and showing it to you on a "paper", I cannot. No matter how many times I try, I cannot.

<br>

Anyway, we are a "[social being](http://conocimientosfundamentales.rua.unam.mx/filosofia/Text/104_tema_06_6.2.2.html)", and if I stay floating in my solitude only with my thoughts, I will probably end up crazy, so I need one way or another to be able to express what I want to say freely, even stupid meaningless things like these.
It contributes nothing to the development of my website, even, I am ~~rea~~ ~~realinti~~ slowing down the progress of this important update. But I like to think that I am making progress in myself.
